Core Analytical Principles

These are the core principles I follow in my work.

Reproducibility
  • End-to-end reproducibility is a fundamental necessity in any analysis, business process or project.
  • The steps to gather, transform and model data should be documented, tested and peer-reviewed.

    • Interpretability
  • It is not enough to simply aggregate data and display summaries for viewers to digest.
  • Valuable analysis provides recommendations backed by statistically sound methods.

    • Automatability
  • The benefits of scalability and consistency from automation are vital to growth and insights.
  • Devoting the resources to develop, test and monitor automated processes should be a priority.
