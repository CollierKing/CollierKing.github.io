Core Analytical Principles

Below are some of the fundamental characteristics I strive to embody in my work.

Reproducibility

  • End-to-end reproducibility is a fundamental necessity in any data analysis, business process or project.
  • The steps to gather, transform and model data should always be clearly annotated and tested to ensure that results are consistent and able to be peer-reviewed.

    • Interpretability

  • It is not enough to simply aggregate data and display summaries for viewers to digest.
  • Valuable anlysis is complete from problem statement to conclusion and provides recommendations backed by statistically sound methods.

    • Automatability

  • The benefits of reproducibility, consistency and cost savings from automation are vital in any business environment.
  • Acquiring the talent and resources to develop, test and monitor automated processes should be a priority.
